# Career Scout Protocol

1. Discover a current official job or dated public hiring signal.
2. Prove India-compatible remote work or funded relocation.
3. Prove the role is early-career.
4. Identify a current recruiting decision-maker, or a founder only for a proven
   company size of at most 50.
5. Include email only when the exact address appears at the cited public URL.
6. Return structured JSON. Omit candidates with unresolved mandatory evidence.
